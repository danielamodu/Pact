### 💼 LinkedIn Post
```text
Excited to announce Pact Protocol — an enterprise protocol for autonomous on-chain subscriptions! 🚀

For years, recurring payments in Web3 required manual monthly wallet confirmations, killing user retention. Built for the UXmaxx Hackathon, Pact leverages EIP-7702 account delegation and Particle Network Universal Accounts to enable seamless, background subscription pulls.

Key Innovations:
• Deterministic Smart Contract Deployments on Arbitrum One & Base Mainnet
• Cryptographically Scoped EIP-712 Session Keys
• Support for both Native ETH & ERC-20 (USDC/USDT) with Safe low-level transfer protection
• On-chain transparency with 1% protocol fee split
```
